# Profitable App Profiles Analysis

An end-to-end **data analysis project** focused on identifying **profitable app categories and business opportunities** using datasets from the App Store and Google Play. The analysis supports **data-driven product and revenue decisions**.

---

## Project Overview

This project explores mobile app datasets to answer key business questions:
- Which app categories attract the most users?
- What categories show strong potential for profitability?
- Where are opportunities for revenue growth and efficiency?

The analysis uses **Python (Jupyter Notebook)** and **Excel-style summaries** such as frequency tables and aggregated metrics.

---

## Objectives

- Clean and preprocess App Store and Google Play datasets
- Analyze category popularity and distribution
- Identify profitable and high-demand app categories
- Create frequency tables and summary statistics
- Provide actionable recommendations for revenue growth

---

## Tools & Technologies

- **Python**
  - Pandas
  - NumPy
- **Jupyter Notebook**
- **MS Excel** (data review & summaries)

---

---

## Key Insights

- Identified high-demand app categories based on user installs
- Highlighted categories with strong monetization potential
- Compared category distributions across platforms
- Provided recommendations for app development and marketing focus
